We help Children become Beyond Measure:

  • Sports Programs

    With our After-school and Summer sports programs, underprivileged children can get physical, learn more about teamwork and have fun!

  • Art Activites

    From theatre productions, to music lessons, to general arts & crafts, we give children a positive creative outlet to express themselves freely.

  • Leadership Programs

    We always strive to grow and feed the ambitions of all children, through our unique leadership programs, run through the kind volunteering of different leaders of industries, we hope to develop leadership skills that can help children reach their goals.

  • Counselling Services

    We provide children a safe place to talk and discuss with our professionals about any issues they may be going through.

  • Coding Programs

    Coding has become the new literacy, so we've created special coding programs that pique their interest and show them the full potential of coding.

  • Tutoring Services

    Through our after-school tutoring services, we provide children with the needed push to achieve all that they can in school and reach their highest educational level.
